Lorem Ipsum (vol.1)
2018/2019



Lorem Ipsum is interested in ideas of chaos, mess, ritual and liminality in relation to language semantics andsomatics, invented characters/egos, and objects used to present and represent an identity. The work is centred around forming the authoress's alter-persona Lorem Ipsum, and is exhibited as an installation piece consisting of Lorem's wardrobe, masks, and various personal objects made through fictional rituals from found objects and materials.

exhibitions:
⭕ Vladimir Nazor gallery, Zagreb, Croatia, 2019
