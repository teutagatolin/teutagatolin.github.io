Elapsing Lorem Ipsum (vol.2)
2019



Volume 2. of Lorem Ipsum series returns again to researching the mercurial natures of chaos, identity, uncanniness and liminality, this time in relation to virtual spaces and archival material from online databases. The work is presented as a virtual environment and snippets of poetry generated from mathematics textbooks found on the Internet Archive (archive.org)




⭕ online exhibition and production by Format C for VR_incubator project, 2019
